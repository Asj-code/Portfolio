About me

my story

I started my career in the computer world as a software tester. After some courses and certifications I realized that I was also very interested in the programming world so I decided to join a full stack web development bootcamp and explore my facet as a developer.

Finally I discovered that I am passionate about both worlds and it is possible to put into play my skills and develop my interests in both areas. So today I am dedicated to both developing and testing applications that improve the lives of those around me.

Even if you don't know me yet, you have to know that I look forward to being part of your project and helping you to be very successful.
